Security Alert (The "New Device Login" Warning)Security events require maximum visibility across all communication channels to ensure the user notices the breach attempt.The Event: A user logs into their account from a new country or unrecognized device.Why all three channels matter:SMS: Delivers a high-priority fraud alert directly to their cellular network.Push Notification: Alerts them instantly if they are actively using their phone.Email: Provides the detailed security logs, IP address, and a "Lock My Account" link.
